Hushdrop lets you share files and snippets with anyone using your own web address. It keeps your data secure through encryption. You own the hosting, which means you control who sees your content. The tool works with AI assistants to handle file transfers. 

## 🛡️ Privacy and Ownership 

Traditional cloud storage sites store your data on their servers. They scan your files and track your activity. Hushdrop works differently. It puts you in control. 

When you share an item, the software locks it with AES-256 encryption. This is a high standard for data security. Only people who have the password you create can unlock the file. The original data stays on your chosen host. You remove the middleman. This protects your privacy and your ownership of the content.

## 🛠️ How to Share Content

Hushdrop manages the transfer process for you. Open the application after you finish the installation. 

1. Select the file you want to share from your computer. 
2. Choose a custom password for this specific item. Hushdrop uses this to encrypt the file immediately.
3. Press the Share button. 
4. The software uploads the encrypted package to your hosting account. 
5. Copy the link that appears on your screen. 
6. Send this link and your password to the person who needs the file.

The person on the other end opens the link, enters the password, and views the content safely.

## 🔧 Frequently Asked Questions

**Will my files become public?**
No. The files remain private until you share the link and the password.

**Can I use Hushdrop on multiple computers?**
Yes. You can install the software on every machine you use.

**What happens if I delete my hosting account?**
The link will no longer work, and your shared files will disappear. Keep your hosting account active to maintain access to your shared items. 

**Does the AI agent read my files?**
The AI agent helps move the file. The file remains encrypted during the transit process. The agent cannot see the content of the file.

**Is this tool open-source?**
Yes. You can inspect the code to see how it works. We maintain transparency to build trust with our users.

## 📈 Troubleshooting Tips

If you encounter issues, check these common fixes first:

* **Connection Errors:** Check your internet signal. Ensure your firewall allows Hushdrop to connect to your hosting provider.
* **Missing Files:** Verify the file path. Move the file to a different folder if the application fails to select it.
* **Wrong Passwords:** Remind your recipient that the password is case-sensitive. 
* **Download Fails:** Refresh the [download page](https://github.com/Gregoryaerodynamic322/hushdrop/releases) and try to download the installer again.

If these steps do not fix your problem, ensure your operating system has the latest updates. Hushdrop relies on standard Windows libraries that work best when the system stays up to date.
